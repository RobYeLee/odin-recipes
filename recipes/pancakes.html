This top-rated banana pancake recipe is easy

Learn how to make, store, and serve the best banana pancakes ever.

Ingredients

  • Flour
  • Sugar
  • Baking Powder
  • Salt
  • An egg
  • Milk
  • Vegetable Oil
  • Bananas

Steps

  1. Combine flour, sugar, salt, baking powder in one bowl and your egg, milk, vegetable oil, mashed bananas in another bowl.
  2. Add the dry ingredients to the bowl with the wet ingredients, then stir until they're incorporated. It's OK if your batter is slightly lumpy.
  3. Pour the batter in Â¼ cup portions onto a lightly oiled pan or griddle over medium-high heat.
  4. Cook for a few minutes, flip with a spatula, and cook for another few minutes (or until each side is golden brown).
  5. Serve your banana pancakes immediately. They're delicious alone or with your favorite pancake toppings.
